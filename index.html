01 About Me

Building reliable software

I'm a Software Engineer embedded in the Team Service Cloud at regiocom SE — one of Europe's leading customer care and IT services providers. My day-to-day work sits at the intersection of backend architecture, workflow automation, and seamless frontend experiences.

I design and build microservices with Java Spring Boot, craft reactive frontends with Angular & TypeScript, and automate complex business processes using Camunda BPM. Beyond the code, I believe great software lives in the intersection of engineering discipline and human empathy — understanding what users actually need and delivering it elegantly.
